F-14 — f14.glb (required for high-quality Tomcat mesh)
The Hugging Face airforce pack does NOT include F-14. Place the file manually:
1. Open https://sketchfab.com/3d-models/f-14-tomcat-top-gun-gear-up-downloadable-9d2d0c87539046aa8c2198fcc47cdcf8
2. Download → glTF Binary (.glb)
3. Save as assets/models/f14.glb
